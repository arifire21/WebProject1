Maine Coons

Introduction

Maine Coons are one of my favorite breeds of cat. The fluffy tail and beautiful longhaired fur draw a lot of people to the breed. I've seen them called "dogs of the cat world," and my own pet cat acts slightly doglike.


image source

Info

The breed gets its name from originating in Maine, though its exact origins are still unknown. Today, though, the maine coon is a popular breed, especially in cat shows.
